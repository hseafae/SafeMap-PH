How It Works
  1. Choose what to do: view the map, submit a report, or get help.
  2. Drop a pin for the location (you can enter a general place only).
  3. Describe what happened (no names or exact addresses).
  4. Admins review and label reports. Approved unverified reports show as heat; verified data powers official analytics.

About SafeMap PH

SafeMap PH is a public-facing awareness tool that separates anonymous community reports from police-verified incidents. Anonymous items appear only as aggregated heat after review. Verified PNP data powers operational dashboards and performance analytics.

Two-Layer Model
Unverified vs. verified data are never mixed. This protects decisions from unvalidated submissions.
AI Assistant
Ask the chatbot about map filters, report steps, and hotline resources any time.
Disclaimer: SafeMap PH does not replace official police reporting. If you are in danger, call the PNP emergency hotline immediately.
